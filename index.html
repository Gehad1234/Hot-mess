About

Hot mess

Ocean acidification is a significant problem that negatively affects the life of marine organisms. The ocean acidification phenomenon occurs due to an increase in the percentage of carbon dioxide in the ocean because water absorbs about 30% of the carbon in the atmosphere, released mainly by factories. How to detect climate change in a specific area and how to apply IoT in transferring data from the pH sensor and co2 detector to our website application is the primary mission.
